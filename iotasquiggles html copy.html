Krasnogyarsk-26 - Jan. 25 2017 [I]

The Russian closed town of Zheleznogorsk (or Krasnogyarsk-26 when the USSR kept it secret), is a town enveloped in the nuclear industry. It was established in the 1950s for the production of weapons-grade plutonium, and the nuclear facilities were built inside caves excavated into the mountain on the northern edge of the town were used during the original Soviet effort to split the atom. Their flag represents Krasnogyarsk-26 quite well - it follows standard Communist design code, with a crimson background and an angular golden bear standing inside of an atom, clawing at an egg-shaped nucleus at its core.

Desegnoj por eldono Esperanto de Harry Potter - Jan. 30 2017 [II]

I found an Esperanto translation of Philosopher's Stone on reddit, downloaded it, and decided I wanted to make some cover art-things for it.

I found the artwork for my two favourite versions (the original UK children's edition and the new UK adult's edition), found a font both compatible with Esperanto's fudgy consonant circumflexes and looked sufficiently Potterish, ('Snell Roundhand') colored the text, italicized and overlayed it onto the originals.
